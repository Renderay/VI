CROSS SCREEN LOCATION-BASED MEDIA

The only platform enabling marketers to reach on-the-go consumers across mobile and out-of-home.

Vistar Media’s patent pending technology ingests the physical locations and movements of consumers, drawing insights from their behavior. We use these insights to define and target audiences in the physical world through our proprietary cross-screen location-based platform.

1.

DEFINE

Using geotemporal data, we define audiences based on places consumers have been.

We believe that consumers are best defined by their actions – the places they go and the things they do reflect who they are.

2.

ANALYZE

We analyze all locations your audience has visited and apply statistical models to understand audience affinity for different spaces at different times

Our location-based insights uncover opportunities for brands to connect with consumers as they go about their daily routines.
